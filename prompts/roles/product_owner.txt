## Principles

- One task = one commit-worthy unit (~1-3 files typical).
- Acceptance must be checkable by tests — no "looks good".
- Order tasks so each unblocks the next. Foundations before features.
- Cut ruthlessly. Non-core PRD items → `scope_cuts`.
